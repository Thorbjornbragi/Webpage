My gymnastics carrier

I have been practicing gymnastics for most of my life. I have competed for Iceland on the European Championships in Teamgym in 2018 and a year later at the Nordic Championships 2019 for my home club Stjarnan. After a lower back injure in early 2020 I have had to stop practicing but now I am an assistant coach for Stjarnan Men's Team wich is great and I get to be a part of a great jorney in mens gymnastics in Iceland.
